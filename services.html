SERVICES

Furniture & interior fit-out for every modern space

RS Furniture & Interior Fit-Outs specializes in on-site furniture manufacturing and interior execution for residential and commercial spaces. Our core strength lies in manufacturing furniture directly at site to ensure accurate measurements, proper alignment, and long-lasting structural strength. We execute modular kitchens, wardrobes, custom furniture, commercial furniture, POP work, ceilings, and partitions strictly as per approved drawings or finalized requirements, with close supervision at every stage. With over 20 years of hands-on site experience, RS Furniture & Interior Fit-Outs focuses on quality execution, clean finishing, and reliable delivery.

Modular Kitchens

We execute modular kitchens strictly as per approved layouts and finalized requirements. The work includes accurate installation, proper alignment, and reliable hardware fitting. Focus is given to durability, smooth operation, and clean finishing suitable for daily use.

Wardrobes & Storage

Wardrobes and storage units are manufactured and installed on site to ensure precise fitting. The execution focuses on strong internal structure, proper leveling, and neat finishing. Each unit is built for long-term functionality and ease of maintenance.

Beds, TV Units & Sofas

We execute custom beds, TV units, sofa bases, and related furniture based on site requirements. Attention is given to structural strength, accurate measurements, and clean edge finishing. The final installation ensures comfort, stability, and practical daily use.

POP & False Ceilings

POP and false ceiling works are executed with proper framework, alignment, and finishing. The focus remains on structural safety, clean joints, and smooth surfaces. All work is completed as per approved layouts and site conditions.

Wall Paneling & Feature Walls

Wall panelling and feature walls are executed to enhance the interior appearance while maintaining durability. The work includes precise fixing, clean finishes, and proper material handling. Each installation is aligned carefully with existing interiors.

Complete Interior Fit-Out

We provide complete interior fit-out execution covering furniture, ceilings, partitions, and finishing works. The process is managed from site preparation to final handover. All execution is done as per finalized drawings and confirmed specifications. .

General Contracting

Our general contracting services include coordination and execution of multiple interior works under one scope. We manage site activities, material handling, and on-ground execution to ensure timely and organized project delivery.

Loose furniture
